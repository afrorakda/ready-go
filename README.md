# #45 Ready Go

Ready Go is a hyper-minimalist "anti-forgetting" tool designed to clear your mind before you step out. Instead of a traditional checklist, it uses a "subtraction" method: you visually eliminate potential anxieties (items) until your mind—and the screen—is completely empty and ready for the world.

## Core Philosophy
* **1 Tool / 1 Action**: Tap to eliminate items. When the screen is empty, you are ready.
* **Subtraction over Addition**: Don't build a list; destroy your anxiety.
* **The "Go" Moment**: Achieving a blank screen provides a psychological "reset" for a confident exit.

## How to Use
1. **Setup**: Select the items you usually carry from the preset grid (Keys, Phone, Wallet, etc.).
2. **Custom Slots**: Use the two text fields for your unique daily needs.
3. **Ready**: Tap "READY TO GO" to scatter your items onto the stage.
4. **Eliminate**: As you put each item in your bag, tap it on the screen. Watch it shake and vanish.
5. **Done**: Once everything is gone, "DONE. GO!" appears. Leave your house with a clear head.

## Design System
* **Background**: #fff (Pure White)
* **Main UI**: #333 (Charcoal Gray)
* **Radius**: 15px (Consistent with Fridge Combo standards)
* **Feedback**: Visual "shake & red flash" effect upon item elimination.

## Privacy & Performance
* **No Login / No Tracking / No Ads**
* **Browser Only**: All data is stored locally in your browser via LocalStorage.
* **Low-Spec Optimized**: Built with lightweight CSS/JS for smooth performance on any mobile device.
